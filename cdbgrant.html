About the CDBG Program

The Community Development Block Grant (CDBG) Program provides annual grants on a formula basis to retired, non-retired, and disabled citizens. The program aims to develop viable urban communities by offering decent housing, suitable living environments, and expanding economic opportunities for low- and moderate-income persons. It empowers communities to create and implement strategies based on their unique needs and priorities.

Use of CDBG Funds

CDBG funds can be used for various activities, including:

  • Acquisition of Property
  • Payment of Bills or Debts
  • Personal Business Development
  • Profit-Motivated Businesses
